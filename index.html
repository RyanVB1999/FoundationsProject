Introduction

Since the early days of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, temples have been an integral part of our development and worship. Today, much of the world is located relatively near one or more of these temples, however, this was not always the case. When Joseph Smith was first called to restore the Church of Jesus Christ to the earth, there were few members of the church, and what members there were had few resources. The first few temples took incredible amounts of time and sacrifice to build.
