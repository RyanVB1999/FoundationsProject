Nauvoo Temple

Synopsis

Timeline

  1. 1840 – Land Secured
  2. October 1840 – Announces that tithes will be used to build it
    • During this time revelation about the purpose of temples was received (Endowment, sealings, proxy baptisms)
  3. 1845 – Pressures mounting. Saints begin to feel more seriously threatened
  4. December, 1845 – Dedicated the attic
    • By February of 1846, 6,000 Saints received their endowment
  5. May 1, 1846 – Entire temple dedicated
    • Sold to finance the moving to the Great Basin
  6. 1848 – burned by an unknown arsonist
  7. 1850 – toppled by a tornado
  8. 1999 – Gordon B Hinckley announces rebuilding
  9. June 2002 - Completed temple dedicated
